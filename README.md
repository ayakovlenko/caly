# caly

Simple command-line calendar.

```sh
$ caly

August 2022

	Mo	Tu	We	Th	Fr	Sa	Su
W31	 1	 2	 3	 4	 5	 6	 7
W32	 8	 9	10	11	12	13	14
W33	15	16	17	18	19	20	21
W34	22	23	24	25	26	27	28
W35	29	30	31
```

Specific month:

```
$ caly -t 2012/12

December 2012

	Mo	Tu	We	Th	Fr	Sa	Su
W48						 1	 2
W49	 3	 4	 5	 6	 7	 8	 9
W50	10	11	12	13	14	15	16
W51	17	18	19	20	21	22	23
W52	24	25	26	27	28	29	30
W1	31
```
